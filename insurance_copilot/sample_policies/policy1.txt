SECTION 1: WAITING PERIODS

1.1 Pre-Existing Conditions:
Any disease, ailment, or condition that was diagnosed or treated within 48 months
prior to policy commencement is considered a pre-existing condition. All pre-existing
conditions are subject to a mandatory waiting period of 30 days from the policy start date.
Claims made within this period will not be entertained.

1.2 Initial Waiting Period:
A general waiting period of 30 days applies from the date of commencement of this
policy. No claims except for accidental injuries will be payable during this period.

1.3 Cancer Treatment:
Coverage for all forms of cancer treatment, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy,
and surgical oncology, is subject to a 90-day waiting period from the policy inception date.

1.4 Maternity Benefits:
Maternity coverage including delivery charges, pre/post natal care, and newborn baby
cover is applicable only after completion of 9 months (270 days) of continuous policy coverage.

---

SECTION 2: ROOM RENT LIMITATIONS

2.1 Daily Room Rent Cap:
The maximum eligible room rent reimbursable under this policy is capped at Rs. 3,000 per
day. Any charges exceeding this limit shall be borne by the insured. If the insured opts
for a room with rent higher than the applicable limit, all associated charges (ICU charges,
surgeon fees, nursing charges) shall be proportionately reduced as per the ratio of
eligible room rent to actual room rent chosen.

---

SECTION 3: EXCLUSIONS

The following treatments and conditions are explicitly excluded from coverage under this policy:

3.1 LASIK Eye Surgery:
Refractive error correction procedures including LASIK, LASEK, PRK, and similar laser
eye surgeries are not covered under this policy, even if medically recommended.

3.2 Dental Treatment:
All dental treatments, procedures, orthodontic work, and dental implants are excluded.
Dental injuries resulting from accidents may be covered — refer to the accidental injury
rider for details.

3.3 Cosmetic and Plastic Surgery:
Any cosmetic, aesthetic, or plastic surgery undertaken for beautification purposes,
unless necessitated by an accident or disease as certified by a registered physician.

3.4 Infertility Treatments:
IVF, IUI, and other assisted reproductive technologies are not covered.

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK HOSPITALS

The following hospitals form part of the approved cashless network:
- Apollo Hospitals (all branches)
- Fortis Healthcare (all branches)
- AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences)

Cashless treatment is available only at network hospitals. For non-network hospitals,
the insured must pay upfront and submit reimbursement claims within 30 days of discharge.

---

SECTION 5: SUB-LIMITS

5.1 Cataract Surgery: Maximum Rs. 25,000 per eye per policy year.
5.2 Ambulance Charges: Limited to Rs. 2,000 per hospitalisation.
5.3 ICU Charges: Capped at 2x the applicable room rent limit per day.

---

SECTION 6: CO-PAYMENT

A co-payment of 10% applies to all claims made at non-network hospitals.
No co-payment is applicable for cashless claims at network hospitals.
